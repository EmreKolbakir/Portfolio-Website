Motivation

Writing professionally can be tricky ‚Äî especially when trying to express emotions like confidence without sounding arrogant. This is even harder for non-native speakers. TonePolish solves that by offering real-time tone-aware rewriting, powered by open-source LLMs.

What It Does

  • Analyzes the emotional tone of your original sentence.
  • Rewrites the input sentence in your desired tone: Friendly, Confident, Humble, or Formal.
  • Outputs the original vs. rewritten text side-by-side.
